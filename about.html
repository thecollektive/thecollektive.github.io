About

“The best political, social, and spiritual work we can do is to withdraw the projection of our shadow onto others.” ~ C J Jung

We are a small autonomous collective with a desire for a world without governance, oppression or hierarchy. We value and support human and animal rights, fair resource sharing & ecological and environmental sustainability

We oppose all forms of prejudice and authoritarianism. We want to promote social advocacy & ownership. Free information sharing, communication with privacy as a right not a privilege. Our support is for all those who are subjugated and oppressed.
Only through radical change and alternative thinking will real difference be made.
