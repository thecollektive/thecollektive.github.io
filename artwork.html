Artwork

Artwork by Everett Thomas


"The only calibration that counts is how much heart people invest, how much they ignore their fears of being hurt or caught out or humiliated. And the only thing people regret is that they didnt live boldly enough, that they didn't invest enough heart, didn't love enough." ~ Ted Hughes

A vast chasm exists between the art that is made and the art that is seen and appreciated. Sometimes there is good reason for this, but often there is not. Our pursuit is that of one which aims to discover and broadcast artists and their works that either never had the opportunity to be appreciated or simply never the platform to broadcast themselves.


We look forward to exhibiting a selection of Everett Thomas' and other under-represented artists work across select venues and galleries accross the Southwest of England. Watch this space for more info and regular artwork and artist updates.
